## Client Responsibilities

The following must be delivered by the client before the project clock starts. Delays caused by late client delivery extend the timeline proportionally.

- Written copy for all pages (or purchase the Copywriting add-on)
- Logo files (SVG or high-resolution PNG)
- Brand guidelines (colors, fonts, tone) or written confirmation that NoDrftSystems should define them
- Photography and video assets (or written confirmation that stock/placeholder is acceptable)
- Domain access credentials
- Hosting account access if client-managed

---

## Universal Exclusions (All Packages)

- Copywriting or proofreading of any written content (unless add-on is purchased)
- Photography, videography, or original illustration
- Logo or brand identity design from scratch
- Modifications to existing WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, or similar platform installations
- Native iOS or Android mobile app development
- AI/ML model training, fine-tuning, or data labeling
- Paid advertising campaign management
- GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, or SOC 2 compliance certification
- Data migration from legacy systems (unless explicitly scoped)
- Ongoing cloud infrastructure management (use retainer)
