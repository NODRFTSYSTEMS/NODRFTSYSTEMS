# Strategy Brief Builder

## Use When

- discovery is complete enough to shape delivery
- a proposal-ready scope needs an internal execution brief
- build work should not begin from fragmented notes

## Required Inputs

- intake packet
- discovery notes or workshop outputs
- package path or commercial context
- constraints, dependencies, and risks

## Workflow

1. Extract verified facts from intake and discovery.
2. Separate scope from assumptions.
3. Define:
   - objective
   - in-scope work
   - exclusions
   - dependencies
   - risks
   - delivery direction
   - acceptance criteria
4. Flag unresolved decisions that still need human approval.

## Outputs

- strategy brief
- scope boundary list
- acceptance criteria
- unresolved-decision list

## Do Not Do

- do not turn vague discovery notes into false certainty
- do not hide unresolved dependencies inside the scope
- do not treat commercial assumptions as approved facts
